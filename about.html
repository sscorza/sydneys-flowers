Our Founder

Sydney Scorza founded Sydney's flowers to provide high quality flowers at a low cost. After being unhappy with current selections, she decided to take matters into her own hands and create a beautiful flower shop. She prides herself in only using the finest flowers.

We'd Love to See You

Located in Chicago, IL, Sydney's flowers is conveniently located near public transportation, as well as within access of major highways. We'd love to see you, come visit!
